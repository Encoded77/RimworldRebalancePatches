[h1]Rebalance Patches[/h1]
Opinionated balance tweaks and cross-mod compatibility patches for my mod list, with the goal of making everything work together as a more cohesive experience. Patches only apply when their required mods are loaded, and every tweak can be enabled or disabled in the mod settings (requires a restart).

Heavily inspired by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3250762483]TooManyMods - Compat[/url] and the Progression series of research overhauls, alongside plenty of my own patches, with everything behind optional settings.

Warning: this mod touches a lot of things. I'll do my best to keep every change documented and toggleable, but with this many patches there's a good chance I've missed something. I'll also keep on adding to it over time as I patch or integrate other mods together.

Here's a list of features:

[h2]Genetics Research Overhaul[/h2]
Inspired by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3239506998]Progression: Genetics[/url], but without [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2801160906]Vanilla Genetics Expanded[/url] because I don't like playing with that mod. Push the genetics tier to spacer tier and rework the full progression from basic genetic sampling to archogenetics. Requires Biotech.

Integrated mods:
[list]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3236303965]ReSplice: Core[/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3016454783]Gene Extractor Tiers[/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3264344552]Gene Nodes - Genes for Sale[/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3352773112]Gene Fabrication[/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3524806362]Gene Ripper[/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3618306875]Vanilla Quests Expanded - Ancients[/url]: a new archogen engineering capstone research lets you build the archogen injector and its lab facilities yourself, at archite-tier costs — recovering them from ancient vaults remains the early route.
[/list]

Each integration has its own toggle; missing mods are simply skipped.

[h2]Genepool Cleanup[/h2]
With a full gene modlist, dozens of genes do the same thing under different names. This module keeps one canonical gene per function and removes the duplicates through [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3521312241]Cherry Picker[/url] — automatically, no Cherry Picker setup needed. Every xenotype that carried a removed gene is rewired to the canonical version, so races keep their identity through shared genes: [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2891845502]Alpha Genes[/url] keeps immunities and armor, [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2920751126]Big and Small[/url] keeps body mechanics, the specialist VRE packs keep their specialty, Det's packs keep their signature quirks.

On top of the deduplication:
[list]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2891845502]Alpha Genes'[/url] ~90 animal summon genes are removed outright (no xenotype uses them).
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2893586390]VRE - Hussar[/url]'s ~300 per-weapon aptitude genes become four aptitudes — light/heavy melee, light/heavy ranged — with the same bonus; hussars still roll a random one, and a new archite gene node sells them (Gene Nodes). This part only needs VRE - Hussar.
[*]Gene conflict fixes: broken and overpowered gene stacks (dodge, claws, deathless, hemogen drain, melee speed, psychic sensitivity...) across [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2920751126]Big and Small[/url], [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2886992038]WVC[/url], [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2891845502]Alpha Genes[/url] and [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/browse/?appid=294100&browsesort=textsearch&section=readytouseitems&p=1&num_per_page=30&days=7&searchtext=Vanille+Race+Expanded]VRE[/url] become mutually exclusive.
[*]Xenotype gene integrations: Some xenotypes get a few extra thematic genes from across the modlist to round out their identity. On the balance side, [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2886992038]WVC[/url]'s most powerful races no longer spawn as random wanderers or faction pawns — they stay obtainable through WVC's own events, morphs and implanters.
[/list]

The deduplication is only active when Alpha Genes, WVC and Big and Small are all loaded; each part has its own toggle, and restoring is as simple as toggling off and restarting. The full list of gene and xenotype changes is documented on github.
