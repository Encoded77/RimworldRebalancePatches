[h1]Rebalance Patches[/h1]
Opinionated balance tweaks and cross-mod compatibility patches for my mod list, with the goal of making everything work together as a more cohesive experience. Patches only apply when their required mods are loaded, and every tweak can be enabled or disabled in the mod settings (requires a restart).

Heavily inspired by TooManyMods - Compat and the Progression series of research overhauls, alongside plenty of my own patches, with everything behind optional settings.

Warning: this mod touches a lot of things. I'll do my best to keep every change documented and toggleable, but with this many patches there's a good chance I've missed something. I'll also keep on adding to it over time as I patch or integrate other mods together.

Here's a list of features:

[h2]Genetics Research Overhaul[/h2]
Inspired by Progression: Genetics, but without Vanilla Genetics Expanded because I don't like playing with that mod. Push the genetics tier to spacer tier and rework the full progression from basic genetic sampling to archogenetics. Requires Biotech.

Integrated mods:
[list]
[*]ReSplice: Core
[*]Gene Extractor Tiers
[*]Gene Nodes - Genes for Sale
[*]Gene Fabrication
[*]Gene Ripper
[*]Vanilla Quests Expanded - Ancients: a new archogen engineering capstone research lets you build the archogen injector and its lab facilities yourself, at archite-tier costs — recovering them from ancient vaults remains the early route.
[/list]

Each integration has its own toggle; missing mods are simply skipped.

[h2]Genepool Cleanup[/h2]
With a full gene modlist, dozens of genes do the same thing under different names. This module keeps one canonical gene per function and removes the duplicates through Cherry Picker — automatically. Every xenotype that carried a removed gene is rewired to the canonical version, so races keep their identity through shared genes: Alpha Genes keeps immunities and armor, Big and Small keeps body mechanics, the specialist VRE packs keep their specialty, Det's packs keep their signature quirks.

On top of the deduplication:
[list]
[*]Alpha Genes' ~90 animal summon genes are removed outright (no xenotype uses them).
[*]VRE - Hussar's ~300 per-weapon aptitude genes become four aptitudes — light/heavy melee, light/heavy ranged — with the same bonus; hussars still roll a random one, and a new archite gene node sells them (Gene Nodes). This part only needs VRE - Hussar.
[*]Gene conflict fixes: broken and overpowered gene stacks (dodge, claws, deathless, hemogen drain, melee speed, psychic sensitivity...) across Big and Small, WVC, Alpha Genes and VRE become mutually exclusive.
[*]Xenotype gene integrations: Some xenotypes get a few extra thematic genes from across the modlist to round out their identity. On the balance side, WVC's most powerful races no longer spawn as random wanderers or faction pawns — they stay obtainable through WVC's own events, morphs and implanters.
[/list]

The deduplication is only active when Alpha Genes, WVC and Big and Small are all loaded; each part has its own toggle, and restoring is as simple as toggling off and restarting. The full list of gene and xenotype changes is documented on github.

[h2]Sci-fi Renaming[/h2]
Renames fantasy-, Norse- and religion-flavoured races and factions to fit RimWorld's sci-fi setting: everything reads as an engineered gene-line, with labels, descriptions, faction names and pawn kinds rewritten to match:
[list]
[*]Big and Small - Races: jotun become gigants, ogres hulkers, dvergr deepkin, nisse minikin, svartalfs umbrakin, redcaps scrappers, trolls regenerants, flesh golems bioconstructs; Muspelheim becomes the Cinderhold Dominion and Niflheim the Permafrost Clans.
[*]Heaven and Hell: angels become ascendants, demons abyssals; the factions become the Luminal Ascendancy, the Abyssal Dominion and the Exiles.
[*]Genes & More, Yokai, Lamias, Slimes, More Xenotypes: allurists, greater impids, reanimates, vulpids, felids and hornbrutes, the serpid family, plasmoids, dreadspiders.
[*]WVC and Alpha Genes: necrokin, psykin, cindrids and abyssids.
[/list]

[h2]Miscellaneous Patches[/h2]
A curated batch of cross-mod fixes and tweaks, each with its own toggle:
[list]
[*]RimIOT - Logistic Matrix: cheaper builds, basic components only, no power consumption.
[*]Altered Carbon: disables the redundant ranged shield belt from VAE Accessories in favour of the harder to get cuirassier belt, and a slider for the casting relay's needlecasting range (default 10 tiles per relay instead of 5).
[*]GiTS Cyberbrains: only basic cyberbrains on the market, surgeries via EPOE-Forked brain surgery, post-basic cyberization at ultratech tier, harsher extreme mental break, and a streamlined research tree.
[*]Odyssey: long-range passenger shuttle — 2000 chemfuel and 2000 kg cargo capacity.
[*]Alpha Genes': genes join vanilla genepacks, alphapacks/mixedpacks removed.
[*]Integrated Implants: slime-safe shoulder turrets, Alpha Mechs chip requirements for mechanitor implant research, levitators ignore water, signal boosters stack with AG command range genes.
[*]Rimsenal: armors and modular weapons gated behind the corp techs; Spacer trade caravans lose their mechanoid guards.
[*]Research prereq cleanups: VFE Pirates warcasket weapons (incl. Warcasket Weapon Quality), Impact Weaponry, Spacer Arsenal; Eltex weapons only on psycasters.
[*]Ideology: inspirations respect precepts, warlike factions can't roll pacifist vows, some modded xenotypes spawn in fitting factions.
[*]Vanilla & DLC: mixed modded mechanoid raid groups, creep joiners accept all human surgeries, gene complexity sliders, curated VQEA archogen injector genes, vacuum resistance nerfs on modded armors (with Vanilla Gravship Expanded).
[/list]
Full details per toggle in the mod settings and in the documentation on github.
