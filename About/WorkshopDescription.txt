[h1]Rebalance Patches[/h1]
Opinionated balance tweaks and cross-mod compatibility patches, this is my attempt to make a cohesive whole out of my modlist. Each patch applies only when the mods it targets are loaded, and every tweak can be toggled on or off in the mod settings (changes take effect after a restart). Requires Harmony.

Heavily influenced by mods like TooManyMods - Compat and the Progression series of mods that rework research trees.
I really liked some of TMM's Patches but since the mod is tailored for his mod list I found myself disagreeing with quite a few tweaks. So I've reimplemented some of his ideas and patches with everything behind toggleable settings, if you don't like some of my design choices, turn them off.

Here's a list of features:

[h2]Genetics Research Overhaul[/h2]
Inspired by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3239506998]Progression: Genetics[/url], but without Vanilla Genetics Expanded because I don't like playing with that mod. Push the genetics tier to spacer tier and rework the full progression from basic genetic sampling to archogenetics. Requires Biotech.

[h2]Compat & rebalances[/h2]
