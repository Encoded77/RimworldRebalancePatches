[h1]Rebalance Patches[/h1]
Opinionated balance tweaks and cross-mod compatibility patches aimed at getting a big pile of mods to play nicely together. Patches only apply when the mods they target are loaded, and every tweak can be turned on or off in the mod settings (restart required).

Heavily inspired by TooManyMods - Compat and the Progression series of research overhauls.

Warning: this mod touches a lot of things. I try to keep every change documented and toggleable, but at this scale I've almost certainly missed something somewhere. It'll also keep growing over time as I patch more mods together. If you ave an issue with a mod try to reproduce withtout this patch enabled.

[h2]Genetics Research Overhaul[/h2]
Inspired by Progression: Genetics, but without Vanilla Genetics Expanded, since I don't enjoy playing with that mod. Genetics moves up to spacer tier and the whole progression gets reworked, from basic genetic sampling up to archogenetics. Needs Biotech.

Integrated mods:
[list]
[*]ReSplice: Core
[*]Gene Extractor Tiers
[*]Gene Nodes - Genes for Sale
[*]Gene Fabrication
[*]Gene Ripper
[*]Vanilla Quests Expanded - Ancients: a new archogen engineering capstone lets you build the archogen injector and its lab facilities yourself, at archite-tier costs. Pulling them out of ancient vaults still works as the early route.
[/list]

Each integration has its own toggle, and anything you don't have installed is skipped.

[h2]Genetics Overhaul[/h2]
Run a full gene modlist and you end up with dozens of genes doing the same thing under different names. This keeps one canonical gene per function and drops the duplicates through Cherry Picker. Any xenotype that carried a removed gene gets pointed at the canonical version instead, so races still read the way they should: Alpha Genes keeps its immunities and armor, Big and Small its body mechanics, the specialist VRE packs their specialty, Det's packs their signature quirks.

On top of the deduplication:
[list]
[*]Alpha Genes' ~90 animal summon genes are removed outright, stopping them from diluting the pool.
[*]VRE - Hussar's used to creates one weapon-aptitude gene per craftable weapon (~300 with a large modlist, diluting the pool) I collapsed them into four aptitudes (light/heavy melee, light/heavy ranged) with the same bonus. They still roll a random one.
[*]Gene conflict fixes: broken and overpowered gene stacks (dodge, claws, deathless, hemogen drain, melee speed, psychic sensitivity...) across Big and Small, WVC, Alpha Genes and VRE become mutually exclusive.
[*]Xenotype gene integrations: some xenotypes pick up a few extra thematic genes from elsewhere in the modlist to round out their identity. On the balance side, WVC's most powerful races no longer spawn as random wanderers or faction pawns; you get them through WVC's own events, morphs and implanters.
[/list]

[h2]Sci-fi Renaming[/h2]
Renames the fantasy, Norse and religion flavoured races and factions into something that fits RimWorld's sci-fi setting. Labels, descriptions, faction names and pawn kinds all get rewritten, so everything reads as an engineered gene-line:
[list]
[*]Big and Small - Races: jotun become gigants, ogres hulkers, dvergr deepkin, nisse minikin, svartalfs umbrakin, redcaps scrappers, trolls regenerants, flesh golems bioconstructs. Muspelheim becomes the Cinderhold Dominion and Niflheim the Permafrost Clans.
[*]Heaven and Hell: angels become ascendants, demons abyssals; the factions become the Luminal Ascendancy, the Abyssal Dominion and the Exiles.
[*]Genes & More, Yokai, Lamias, Slimes, More Xenotypes: allurists, greater impids, reanimates, vulpids, felids and hornbrutes, the serpid family, plasmoids, dreadspiders.
[*]WVC and Alpha Genes: necrokin, psykin, cindrids and abyssids.
[/list]

[h2]Expertise Overhaul[/h2]
Vanilla Skills Expanded and its add-ons offer around seventy expertises, most of them a single narrow stat, and their bonuses scale to absurd numbers by expertise level 20. This replaces that list with 32 broader ones, two to four per skill.
[list]
[*]Bonuses become multipliers topping out around +40% at expertise level 20, instead of flat offsets that reach +100% or overshoot a stat's ceiling entirely. Combat and stat-heavy expertises are tuned below that.
[*]Quality expertises cost work speed: Virtuoso, Master builder and Artificer produce better work more slowly, against Prolific artisan, Siteworker and Fabricator going the other way.
[*]New mechanitor expertises (Mechlord, Mechwright) and psycast expertises (Overchanneler, Quietist)
[*]Expertises from Alpha Skills, Hauts' Framework, Vanilla Fishing Expanded and Vanilla Gravship Expanded are folded in, and stats from Integrated Implants, Mechanoid Upgrades, Altered Carbon and Vanilla Psycasts Expanded ride along when those are loaded.
[/list]
Pawns keep any expertise they already picked.

[*]GiTS Cyberbrains: only basic cyberbrains on the market, surgeries via EPOE-Forked brain surgery, post-basic cyberization at ultratech tier, harsher extreme mental break, and a tidied-up research tree.

[*]Alpha Genes: genes join vanilla genepacks, alphapacks/mixedpacks removed.
[*]Integrated Implants: slime-safe shoulder turrets, Alpha Mechs chip requirements for mechanitor implant research, levitators ignore water, signal boosters stack with AG command range genes.
[*]Rimsenal: armors and modular weapons gated behind the corp techs. Spacer trade caravans lose their mechanoid guards.

[*]Vanilla & DLC: mixed modded mechanoid raid groups, creep joiners accept all human surgeries, gene complexity sliders, a hand-picked gene list for the VQEA archogen injector, vacuum resistance nerfs on modded armors (with Vanilla Gravship Expanded).
[/list]
Full details per toggle in the mod settings and in the documentation on github.
